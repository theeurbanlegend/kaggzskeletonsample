Information

Kagumo High School is a prestigious school located on the cool mountains of Nyeri county in Kiganjo. Its inception was in 1933 as a primary institution in Gatitu.The school has remarkably grown from an artisan institute to a prestigious school renown for its high performance postage streak.

It has a total of 100 teachers and growing. The teachers enrolled usually perform 'teaching practice' and are commonly referred to as tp>teepee according to local slang/sheng.'

Curriculum and method of teaching

The school is currently delivering education under the 8-4-4 system but is slowly adapting to the new ECDE curriculum or CBC. The school has evolved from using chalkboards to whiteboards which has greatly improved the learning environment since the asthmatic students are no longer in fear of chalk dust.
